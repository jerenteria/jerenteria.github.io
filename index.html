Music Room
Player

Designed a music room app that lets you connect your Spotify playlist via Spotify API. You can join a room through a random generated code and listen to music with people that got the same code. The app also lets users skip the songs when it reaches a certain amount of votes to skip. The host of the room sets the amount of votes needed to skip a song when creating the room.
